Deon Menezes

After collecting more “let’s just be friends” than gym memberships, Deon Menezes did what any Indian guy with a broken heart and full ego would do:

He stopped chasing “seen at 2:14 AM” and started building something that actually replies.

While his chats were stuck on double-tick archaeology, his ambition went full IPL powerplay.

Every “you’re such a nice guy” became one more late night, one more plan, one more comeback.

At some point, heartbreak stopped being pain and became fuel: chai-powered, ego-driven, and dangerously consistent.
